About

The Annual Juried Show features selected artists covering a wide-range of media and themes. Exhibiting artists, chosen through a selective juried process, hail from all over the southeast, including South Carolina, North Carolina, and Georgia. The public is invited to participate by casting their votes for the People's Choice Award. The opening reception and awards ceremony is free and open to the public.
